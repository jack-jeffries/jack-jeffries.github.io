Jack Jeffries



I am an Assistant Professor in the Mathematics Department at The University of Nebraska-Lincoln. Before that, I was an Investigador Titular A (tenure-track faculty) in the pure math group at CIMAT, and an NSF postdoc in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Michigan. I completed my Ph. D. under the direction of Professor Anurag K. Singh at the University of Utah; my area of math research is Commutative Algebra. You can find me in Avery Hall 325.
